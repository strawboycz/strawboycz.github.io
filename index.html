PARKOURDLE

How to play?

    💻 I reccomend playing on PC or a laptop for better experience

💡 DOUBLECLICK on the play icon when you are ready to play!

🎥 Parkour video will be played, it will dissapear just before the athlete takes off
be ready, it will be quick!

❓ Your goal is to then guess correctly which trick is the athlete going to do
(Kong precise, Backflip, Cork, ...)

✍ There is an existing dataset of answers, you can type into the text area, 
but please select one of our premade trick names, any other input will not work

👨‍🔬 Please mind the fact that this is a demo and the game is not perfect
